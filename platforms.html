Operating System Details

AIX
SLURM support for AIX is anticipated in the second quarter of 2004.

Linux
SLURM has been thoroughly tested on Red Hat Linux using both IA32 and IA64. We anticipate SLURM functioning properly on other Linux distributions as well.

Interconnect Details

Ethernet
Ethernet requires no special support from SLURM and has been thoroughly tested.

IBM Federation
SLURM support for IBM's Federation Switch is anticipated in the second quarter of 2004.

Infiniband
Infiniband has not been tested with SLURM, but we expect this work to be completed sometime in 2004 with minimal effort.

Myrinet
Myrinet requires no special support from SLURM and has been thoroughly tested.

Other
SLURM ports to other systems will be gratefully accepted.

Quadrics Elan
SLURM support for Quadrics Elan 3 and Elan 4 switches have been thoroughly tested.
